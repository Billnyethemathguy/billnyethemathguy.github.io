Brian's advice on catching a football as a goalkeeper

Welcome to my website. This website will teach you about one of many techniques that should be used to improve your goalkeeping techniques in football

Requirements

  • Goalkeeper gloves
  • Football
  • Friend to shoor the football

Steps to catch the ball

  1. Position yourself in the center of the goal
  2. Dive towards the direct of the ball, if it is moving towards your direct, stay in place.
  3. If the ball is going towards you, create a V-shape with your hands towards the ball, if not reach your arms out towards to ball
  4. Keep your eyes on the ball and make small adjustments based on the direction of the ball
  5. Stay focused and catch the ball
